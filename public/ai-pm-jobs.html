How to land it

The AI PM hiring playbook.

The single biggest accelerator: a portfolio of shipped AI work. Hiring managers skim LinkedIn, read one project case study, and decide in under 5 minutes. Make that project case study strong.

Step 1 · Build the portfolio

Ship 4–6 AI projects: a RAG app, an eval harness, an agent, a cost/quality analysis, a safety red-team, and a full AI PRD. The free 60-day course produces all six.

Step 2 · Write the AI-PM-specific resume

One bullet per AI project. Lead with the product outcome, not the tech. Bad: "Used GPT-4 for summarization." Good: "Shipped RAG-powered doc search; eval-measured relevance improved 34%; cost $0.002/request."

Step 3 · Target warm paths

Cold apply rates at frontier labs are ~1%. Warm intros via working AI PMs, open-source contributions, and community participation are dramatically higher.

Step 4 · Prepare for the AI PM loop

Product sense + AI case study + technical depth + execution + behavioral. Full interview prep guide →

Step 5 · Negotiate with market data

Use the salary ranges above as anchors. For equity-heavy offers, model P50, P10, and P90 outcomes — don't just use the recruiter's "assumed growth" number.

FAQ

AI PM jobs FAQ.

How much do AI Product Managers make?▼
See the salary table above. Entry $160k–$220k base, senior $220k–$320k, principal $280k–$450k, in US. Total comp at frontier labs is 2–4× base with equity.
Which companies hire the most AI PMs?▼
Anthropic, OpenAI, Google DeepMind, Scale, Cohere, Mistral, Hugging Face, Microsoft AI orgs, Meta AI, and AI-native unicorns like Perplexity, Cursor, Glean, Harvey.
Do I need prior AI experience?▼
For frontier labs, typically yes. For AI-native startups and AI teams at big tech, strong PM experience + demonstrable AI fluency (portfolio + shipped labs) is often enough.
Can I transition from engineering/data science?▼
Yes, and this is one of the strongest paths in. Technical depth is already there — focus on building product instincts, writing PRDs, and practicing PM interview formats.
Remote or in-person?▼
Mixed. Most frontier labs are hybrid (SF/NYC/London), most AI-native startups are hybrid or remote-first. Filter on the live job board.
