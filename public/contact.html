Frequently Asked Questions
Common Questions
Is the course really free?▼
Yes — all 60 days are completely free. Sign in with Google, Apple, LinkedIn, or email and get instant access to the full course. No credit card required, ever.
Who is this course for?▼
Anyone working in or transitioning into AI product management. Whether you're a PM at a tech company, an aspiring AI PM, or an engineer looking to understand the PM perspective — this course covers everything from MCP and A2A protocols to Claude Code, evals, and red-teaming.
What do I need to get started?▼
Just a browser. The code labs run Python and JavaScript directly in your browser via WebAssembly — no local setup, no installs, no API keys required.
Can I track my progress?▼
Yes — once you sign in, your progress is saved automatically. Complete days, check off tasks, and add personal notes. Your progress syncs across devices.
I found a bug or have a suggestion. How do I report it?▼
Use the contact form above — select "Bug Report" or "Feature Request" as the subject. Reports are automatically tracked in our GitHub project board for fast resolution.
How long does each day take?▼
Each day is designed for about 60–90 minutes of focused work: reading the context, completing 4 tasks (15–30 min each), running the code lab, and reviewing the interview question.
