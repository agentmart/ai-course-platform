Frequently Asked Questions
Common Questions
Is the course really free?▼
Yes — the first 15 days are completely free with no credit card required. Paid plans unlock all 60 days, the job board, community access, and more.
What do I need to get started?▼
Just a browser. The code labs run Python and JavaScript directly in your browser via WebAssembly — no local setup, no installs, no API keys required.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?▼
Absolutely. You can cancel from your billing portal at any time. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period.
How long does each day take?▼
Each day is designed for about 60–90 minutes of focused work: reading the context, completing 4 tasks (15–30 min each), running the code lab, and reviewing the interview question.
What are PM OS, RIA OS, and ML OS?▼
These are AI operating systems we’re building for specific roles — product managers (PM OS), registered investment advisors (RIA OS), and ML engineers (ML OS). They’re currently in development and will be available soon.
I have a technical issue. How do I report it?▼
Email us at hello@becomeaipm.com with details of the issue (browser, what you were doing, any error messages), or open a GitHub issue for faster resolution.
