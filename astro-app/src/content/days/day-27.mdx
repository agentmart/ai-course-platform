context: "<p><strong>Agent Communication Protocol (ACP)</strong> was developed by the BeeAI team at IBM Research and released as an open standard in 2025. In practice, many enterprises aren’t choosing between these protocols — they’re using MCP for tool connectivity and evaluating A2A vs ACP based on their cloud provider allegiance.</p>"
